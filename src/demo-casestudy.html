When the assignment goes offline

Leave room for the work.

A translator gets a pericope assignment and drops off the grid for weeks. They package every reference — images and source audio — onto one cheap phone. But that same phone has to hold their oral drafts and revisions, recorded in the field. Every megabyte spent on resources is a megabyte they can't record into. So the resources have to be small.
